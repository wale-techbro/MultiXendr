03

What is gas fees?

+

Gas fees are a small charge required by the DApp to process your transaction. They cover the cost of the services provided by Multixender.

04

How are platform fees calculated?

+

Platform fees are determined based on the number of addresses you're sending tokens to. The fee increases incrementally depending on how many recipients. For 1-10 only 1% platform fees, for 11-25 it is 2%, for 25-50 it is 3%, for 50-100 it is 4% and for 100+ the platform fees is 6%.
